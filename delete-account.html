Before You Delete

Please review the following information before submitting your account deletion request:

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, transaction history, wallet information, and card details will be permanently removed from our systems.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile and personal information
  • Transaction history and records
  • Wallet data and saved addresses
  • Card information (physical and virtual)
  • Notification preferences and settings
  • Waitlist and subscription data

What You Should Do First

  1. Withdraw all funds — Transfer any remaining digital assets from your LEO wallet to an external wallet before requesting deletion.
  2. Save transaction records — Download or screenshot any transaction history you may need for tax or personal records.
  3. Cancel active cards — If you have any active physical or virtual cards, ensure they are deactivated.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. During this period, your account will be deactivated. Some data may be retained for up to 90 days as required by regulatory and legal obligations.
